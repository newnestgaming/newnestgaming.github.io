Frequently Asked Questions

My progress was lost. Can I recover it?

Your game data is automatically saved to iCloud (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android). Make sure you're signed in to the same account. If the issue persists, contact us via email with your device and OS version.

The game crashes or freezes. What should I do?

Try restarting the app and make sure your device is running the latest OS version. If the problem continues, email us with a description of when the crash occurs.

How do I delete my account and data?

You can request account deletion here: Request Deletion

Is the game free to play?

Yes! Endless Shuffle Defense is free to download and play.
